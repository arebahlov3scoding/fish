Founder of Nguju Fishery

Mary Wambui Gichangi is the founder of Nguju Fishery Consultation Services. A Maseno University graduate in Aquatic Resources Conservation and Management with IT, her passion is to promote food sustainability across all communities.

Founder's Philosophy and Vision

Philosophy: Promote CSR in fish production and raise awareness about fish farming as a profitable venture.

Vision: Revolutionize aquaculture to boost ROI and advance sustainable fish farming.

About Us

At Nguju Fishery Consultation Services, we offer expert guidance in fish farming practices. Our countrywide service is driven by a committed team that ensures prompt delivery and customer satisfaction. Based in Nakuru, Kenya.
